Hummingbirds are small birds that have super fast wings, hence where they get the "humming" in their name. They have phenominal vison, which is how they find their food. When they are feeding, they move their tongue around 14 times per second, making them very fast eaters! There are also over 330 different kinds of hummingbirds, and yet despite there being so m any theyre so special to interact with.

Seals

seals are chubby, adorable creatures that are in the Northwest Atlantoc. They are mammals, and eat fish. Seals have many whiskers used for touch, similar to why cats have them. And the sounds they make are absolutely adorable.

Arctic Foxes

These adorable foxes have a thick fur coat that keeps them at a nice warm temperature fit for their surroundings. They also use their tails as blankets, and their feet are like built in snow boots. They eat insects, berries, pretty much anything.

Poison Dart Frogs

These frogs are beautiful frogs, but highly dangerous. these frogs use their colors as a warning for them to not be eaten, or else they poison their predators. Like any other frog, they eat insects. They live in South America.
