Here I will talk about some semantic tags we learned, and some I reasearched on my own.

  • a Header, typed as h1-h6. a healer simply adds large text at the top of a page, getting smaller depending on the number. See the top of my doc for an example.
  • a Footer, typed as footer, used right above this vocabulary. it adds a small sentence to the bottom of a page, the best example would be a copyright notice and the date at the bottom of an article.
  • the Nav, typed as nav, lets you open a seperate page on the same website, by including a link.
  • details, typed as details, lets you open and close a box with additonal text. say you're looking up a word, you can click details to get an example of that word.
  • mark, typed mark, adds highlights to a word or sentence and can change the color of the text itself.
